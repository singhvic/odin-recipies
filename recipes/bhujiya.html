Bhujiya

Description

Bhujiya or in this case Aalo bhujiya is a dish cooked almost daily in Bihar. It is easy and quick to prepare and goes well with Dal-Chawal, roti or puri.

If a guest arrives then Puri-Bhujiya will always be served.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes - 4
  • Mustard Oil - 2 Tbsp
  • Red chillies - 2
  • Mustart seeds- 0.5 tbsp

Steps

  1. Dice the potatoes in thin and long french fries like shape
  2. Heat Mustard oil in a pan till you see light from it
  3. Add the Mustard seeds followed by the red chillies snapped in half
  4. Add the diced potatoes and stir
  5. Keep the flame medium
  6. Stir periodically till potatoes are semi cooked
  7. Turn the flame slightly high and let it get crispy
  8. Serve with Chapati, Poori or Dal-Rice
